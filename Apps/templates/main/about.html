WHAT IS COMINDER?

Cominder is a solution to the lazy folks out there.

Ever tried establishing a new habit with Todoist? Or Microsoft To-do?

Sick of having to enter in "do the laundry" every single time?

Well, with Cominder's built in list of tasks, users can add these habits to their daily schedule with one click of a button.

Having to do the same task over and over again, but it's not on the list? We give you the ability to add your own custom tasks to the list.

We also offer a "What's in my Fridge?" feature, where users can choose a list of recipes and automatically generate a shopping list with the needed ingredients.

We have so much more to offer in the coming future, so be prepared!
